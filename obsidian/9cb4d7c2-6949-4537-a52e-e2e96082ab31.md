There are three general kinds of feature-set control: Early-project control of defining a feature set that is consistent with your project's schedule and budget objectives Mid-project control of controlling creeping requirements Late-project control of trimming features to meet a schedule or cost goal. Successful projects learn to use all three kinds of feature-set control to their advantage.
